Privacy

Overview

I use Clicky to track basic information about visitors to this site. I am not logging any information other than what Clicky is tracking.

Visitor data tracked

When a visitor views a page on this site, the following data is sent to the Clicky tracking servers and logged to my account:

  • Page URL
  • Page title
  • Referrer
  • User agent
  • IP address
  • Language
  • Screen resolution

More information on Clicky's privacy practices can be found in their Terms of Use. Also note that I have disabled Clicky's cookies by using their customization instructions
